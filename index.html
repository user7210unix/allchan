8kun, the successor to 8chan, is an imageboard platform emphasizing free speech. Key boards include /qresearch/ (conspiracy theories), /pnd/ (politics and news), and /v/ (video games). It focuses on alternative viewpoints, political discussions, and fringe topics, often attracting users seeking unmoderated spaces.

It features boards like /tech/ (programming and hardware), /cyb/ (cybersecurity), /lamer/ (general tech), and /anime/. Discussions center on coding, hacking, tech culture, and anime, appealing to enthusiasts and professionals alike.
